Bosses

Main bosses

There are a total of 19 bosses in the game and all of them are very difficult to defeat wspecially if you do not know what you are doing. It also matters how you defeat them and in what order becuse it affects the story. If you used the help of one of the friends you acquired through the story it would make it easier on top of going through that branch of the story. So you have to think carfully when deciding what to do with almost every action.

Mini Bosses

The mini bosses are just a bunch of characters that are hard to beat but have little to do with the main story. Except they all give you either a lot of money of some special item. That item usually leads to onother boss or its a very powerfull weapon or peice of armor. Which in turn those items ultimatly affect your game play but if you jsut decide to not fight them there will be no significant impact to the game.

Conclusion

So in the end the bosses can either make or break the game. They can be too hard to beat and you give up or they're too easy and you lose interest. It is very difficult to acquire the right level so that the game is properly balanced and fun. I belive that this game is on the difficult side but thats also what makes the game so entertaining.
