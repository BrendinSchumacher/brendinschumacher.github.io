<h2>The Game</h2>
<p>Dark Souls is a game that is set in the third person perspective and players 
are given a variety of weapons to fight against the enemies. There are also 
bonfires which serve as check points where the player cn save the game and 
refil their health.</p>

<h2>Plot</h2>
<p>It is set in the Kindom of Lorthic and a bell has rung to signal that the 
first flame is dying out. The bell is the last hope for the age of fire and
the lords of cinder (the ones who are supposed to keep the fire going) abandon
their thrones. Then enters the main character (you) and your job is to return
all of the lords back to their thrones. The only way to get them to come back
is defeaing them in battle.</p>

<h2>Sales</h2>
<p>In Japan it <b>sold over 200,000 copies</b> in its first two weeks of rlease. Then 300
million copies were distributed among the world and it was the best selling video
game in the US in a month of the release.</p>
